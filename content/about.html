About

Hello and welcome! I'm Jazz, born and raised in the vibrant city of Kuching. With an MEng (Hons) in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from the University of Nottingham, after years of shaping AI solutions across industry and government, I took the leap in 2026 to found Compute Systems & Solutions (CSS) Sdn. Bhd. — bringing my passion for machine learning, LLMs, and digital transformation into a company built to empower Sarawak's tech future. As Founder, I lead the product engineering and AI innovation roadmap.

Beyond CSS, I also serve as Board of Directors & Shareholder at HBSS Management Sdn. Bhd. — an Investment Holding Company — further expanding my entrepreneurial footprint into the world of investment and business development.

But there's more to me than just work. I'm passionate about staying active! I recharge by swimming, trekking scenic hiking trails, and working out at the gym. Music also plays a big role in my life, allowing me to express creativity through piano and guitar. Plus, I love exploring new horizons through my YouTube music channel, where I share original compositions and creative music projects. Thanks for visiting my site, and I hope your day is filled with inspiration and discovery!
