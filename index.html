Project Title: Smart Sun Tracking System

  • The smart Sun Tracking System project develops a PV panel system that automatically follows the sun's position for optimal energy collection. Using solar energy technology, it converts sunlight into electricity efficiently without environmental impact. The system monitors the sun's position to achieve greater efficiency than standard fixed PV panels.
  • After analyzing various tracking systems and considering Malaysia's equatorial position, we selected a single-axis PV tracker with tilted axis design. The system achieves an annual output of 21.1MWh with 1908 kWh/kWp/year production, suitable for residential use. Our simulation model validates the system's effectiveness for residential power generation.
